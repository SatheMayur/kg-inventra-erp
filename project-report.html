Project Overview
FG Inventory is a production-grade, web-based ERP system built for a food & grains wholesale business. It replaces manual stock registers with a real-time intelligence platform — barcode-driven flows, FIFO expiry tracking, AI-generated operational insights, and an EPR compliance module.

Section 02
Before the Build
The state of inventory management before FG Inventory was implemented — manual processes, fragmented records, zero visibility into stock health or compliance obligations.
Stock Management
Manual register entries · No batch tracking · FIFO not enforced · Stock counts done weekly by hand · Expiry dates tracked on paper · No reorder triggers
Inward / Outward
Excel sheets per vendor · No PO linkage · Manual challan writing · No confirmation or lock process · Corrections by erasing entries · Zero audit trail
Reporting & Visibility
No real-time dashboard · Month-end reconciliation only · Margin calculated manually in Excel · No dead stock identification · No vendor performance data
Compliance & Access
No user roles or access control · Anyone could edit any record · EPR obligations unknown · No audit log · No barcode scanning — all entry manual
Key Pain Points vs. Impact
Pain PointRoot CauseBusiness ImpactSeverity
Stock spoilage No FIFO enforcement, expiry not tracked per batch Direct write-off loss on expired goods Critical
Stockouts No reorder triggers; stock counted weekly Lost sales, emergency purchases at high cost Critical
Reconciliation errors Manual challan ↔ invoice ↔ register mismatch Revenue leakage, vendor disputes Critical
No audit trail Single Excel file, anyone edits Fraud risk, no accountability Critical
EPR non-compliance PWM Rules 2022 obligations unknown Regulatory penalty risk High
No margin visibility Dispatch rate vs purchase rate never compared Pricing decisions made blind High
No barcode scanning All entry manual, high error rate Slow operations, data inaccuracy High
